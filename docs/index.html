Privacy Policy

The Stream Marker for YouTube plugin utilizes the Chrome DevTools Protocol (CDP) to communicate with your local web browser. This communication is strictly local (localhost / 127.0.0.1) and is required solely to trigger the "Insert Marker" button within the YouTube Studio webpage.

  • No personal information is collected.
  • No analytics are tracked.
  • No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.

All operations happen securely on your own device.
